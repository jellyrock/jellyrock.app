JellyRock respects your privacy. This policy describes what information, if any,
this website (jellyrock.app) collects.

## What we collect

This website uses a privacy-respecting analytics tool self-hosted at
analytics.jellyrock.app. It:

- Does **not** use cookies
- Does **not** collect or store personal data
- Does **not** track you across websites
- Does **not** build user profiles
- Is GDPR-compliant without requiring a consent banner

Anonymized, aggregated information is recorded such as page views, referrers,
browser type, operating system, and country. This data is used only to understand
which pages are useful and helps guide project priorities.

## What we do not collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any other personal data
on this website. We do not run advertising. We do not sell or share data with third parties.

## The JellyRock Roku app

This privacy policy covers only this website. The JellyRock Roku app is a separate
product; it communicates directly with your chosen Jellyfin server and does not send
any data to jellyrock.app.
